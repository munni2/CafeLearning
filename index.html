THE WANIASHAN MISSION

REAL INGREDIENTS. REAL PEOPLE. REALLY GREAT SAUCE.

At Wanjashan, we keep it real. Our goal is simple - to share our passion. Pairing nature's nutritious goodness with knowledge passed down to us by buddhist monks, we delicately craft our products for our global partners and you.
We don't replicate a product, we masterfully craft a culinary experience that anyone can enjoy. We are the real deal. No GMOs or shortcuts are taken to merely throw something on the shelves. What began as one man's journey has turned into so much more than sauce. It's about community and breaking bread.
